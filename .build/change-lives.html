    How you can make a difference.

    Every time a World Nomad buys travel insurance, they can choose to add a micro-donation to support a community project and help change lives.

    So far, over 900,000 World Nomads have raised more than $2 million for 121 projects.

    We believe in the power of travel to change lives. Everyone can choose to give a little back to the places & people that they visit.

    Small enough to change the world.

    Small

    A billion people live on less than $2 a day. For less than the price of a cup of coffee, you too can create change and opportunity.

    Easy

    Creating meaningful change has never been so easy; all you have to do is choose which project to help fund when you get a quote.

    Transparent

    100% of funds raised go to your chosen project. All administration and technology are paid by World Nomads as our commitment to you.
