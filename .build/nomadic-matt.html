    When should I buy World Nomads travel insurance?

    Any time you are leaving your country of residence, it's a good idea to have travel insurance.

    • If you want to travel knowing if something goes wrong, you have 24/hr support, then travel insurance is for you.
    • If you're pre-purchasing flights and accommodation, you'll want to insure these should you be forced to cancel.
    • If you're heading off on a big adventure and think you might want to stay longer, then you need insurance that will support this.
    • If you're planning to do any adventure sports or activities on your trip like scuba diving, skiing, zip lining and more, you'll want to make sure you're covered.
    • If you're carrying digital cameras, iPads, laptops or your favourite tech, you'll need insurance cover if something unforeseen happens to your gear.
