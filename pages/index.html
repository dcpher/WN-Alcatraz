Eligibility

I have food restrictions / I am vegetarian. Can I still apply?
The winners of this program will be expected to try everything. If you are up for that, feel free to apply.
I have food allergies. Can I apply?
Yes, feel free to apply, however, should you win it will be your responsibility to manage your own allergies as we cannot ensure that meals / food are managed to your needs.
I am Sri Lankan. Can I participate?
Yes, of course Sri Lankans are eligible too!
Are chef/restaurateur's allowed to apply?
Yes, feel free to apply!
By "18+ years of age", would this be "18 before the deadline" or "18 before the actual trip"?
You do need to be 18 at the time of the application deadline (before March 6, 2015).
I speak only a little English, is this a problem?
English does not have to be your first language, but you do need to have conversational English for this scholarship. In order for you to get the most of the assignment you will need to be able to understand and converse with your guide, the locals and the filmmaker on the trip.

Application

Can I enter more than once?
No, you can only submit one recipe per person – make it a good one!
I have a great recipe, but its not my own. Can I still enter with that recipe?
Yes you can, we don't expect these recipes to be created by you, but rather recipes that you have encountered on your travels - so naturally they will most likely be learned or borrowed from someone else.
Do I need to provide a recipe for an entire meal, or just one dish?
Please keep your recipe to one dish - though the dish could have multiple parts, for example the recipe for making pasta and then the recipe for the sauce that accompanies it.
Can it be a recipe that has already published on my personal food and travel website or does it have to be a previously unpublished one?
Yes, it can be a recipe that you have already published, if you think it is the best one to submit.
Does the recipe entered need to be a Sri Lankan recipe?
It can be any recipe you like from anywhere in the world; it just needs to have a great story behind it.
Which measuring unit should I use for the recipe?
Feel free to put measurements in whatever unit you like.
I am considering submitting a recipe with a seasonal/regional ingredient, are the recipes going to be tested by the judges? Can I still submit my recipe?
The judging panel will test the top recipes. However, we won't discourage you from submitting a recipe with seasonal or regional ingredients. Where possible, try to include a substitute in your recipe if you think it would not be available elsewhere.
Would the quality of the photo affect the judging? Do the photos need to be for each step?

Great photos are always nice, but they don't have a major bearing on the judging process. You don't need to provide a photo of each step (though you can), a couple shots of the process and finished product work just fine.

The photos you submit will automatically be resized; the biggest image possible is 960 pixels wide at 72dpi. Remember: we don’t want more than 5 photos.

The Trip

How will you match the winners to the region?
The winners will be matched to the regions by the organizers prior to the trip.
Are the trip dates final or could they be altered a little?
Yes, these dates are final.
Will there be a guide or translator on the trip travelling with me?
There will be an English-speaking guide with you at all times. The guide will be able to translate in each destination you visit.
What’s the deal with the filmmaker?
A filmmaker will be along to capture all of your food experiences. They will be rolling on everything you eat and cook, and all of your local interactions. You MUST be comfortable with this, they will be your shadow on the assignment.
Can I bring a travel companion or my partner along for the trip?
The three trips are only for one person each. Should you wish to extend that trip in Sri Lanka after the assignment, this can be discussed.
Is there any other way to get into the program besides the scholarship? Can I pay for my room & board myself and take part in the program?
No, unfortunately, you cannot pay your way onto this trip..

Troubleshooting

I have a problem submitting my application. Can I email my entry to you? Help!

No, you cannot email your entry to us. All entrants need to fill the application page and submit their applications via our website.

If you have encountered any specific issues please contact us at scholarships@worldnomads.com
I'm not sure if my application was submitted correctly. How do I check this?
If your entry is complete, you can see your photos in the list here along with everyone else's entries.
