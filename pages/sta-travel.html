When should I buy

travel insurance

STA Travel has teamed up with World Nomads to provide cover for Non UK residents and travellers already overseas because:

  • If you're pre-booking flights, accommodation or tour activities and are worried about being well out of pocket if you need to unexpectedly cancel your trip.
  • If you're concerned about having to cover large medical bills and organise your treatment overseas without the support of a 24/hr emergency assistance team.
  • If you don't want the risk of having to pay upwards of USD $200,000 in evacuation costs should you get injured or seriously ill overseas.
  • If you're worried about replacing your favourite tech such as laptops, digital cameras and iPads if they get stolen when travelling.
  • If you want to have the flexibility to extend your cover should you choose to stay a little longer.

Having travel insurance in place gives you the peace of mind that if something does happen, you're covered!
