What we cover

World Nomads offers two travel insurance plans for Australian independent and adventurous travellers.

With travel insurance coverage for everything you need and nothing you don’t, we’ll make sure you’re covered, even if you run out of travel insurance on the road. We don't just keep you insured, with us you'll travel smarter and safer.

Gear cover for the digital nomad

World Nomads travel insurance doesn't just cover your adventure activities, but as digital nomads ourselves, we know how important it is to have cover for your tech gear as well.

We cover theft or accidental damage of your gear and you can also upgrade your cover to increase the individual item limit to $3,000 per item for such things as;

Camera cover

We have cover for all your photography gear:

Beginner

Our Standard plan covers $500 which is suitable for a pocket point-and-shoot.

Enthusiast

Our Explorer plan covers $2,000 for all of your combined cameras and lenses which is suitable for an entry level camera.

Semi-Pro

You can increase cover for your combined cameras and lenses up to $6,000 with a per item limit of $3,000 by adding these to your policy.

iPhone & iPad cover

Our Standard Plan covers up to $2,000 with a per item limit of $500. our Explorer Plan covers up to $10,000 with a per item limit of $2,000.

Laptop cover

Our Standard Plan covers up to $2,000 with a per item limit of $500. our Explorer Plan covers up to $10,000 with a per item limit of $2,000.
